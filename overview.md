# Overview

## What This Wiki Covers

This wiki tracks the **CaseWare Probe How-To Document Project** — a structured effort to build a complete library of professional training guides for the [[entities/probe-audit-premium-plus]] audit methodology as implemented within [[entities/caseware-working-papers]]. The project was self-initiated by Wes as both a personal learning tool and a resource for other audit clerks. All finished materials are published on the [[entities/success-community]] platform and YouTube.

## The Four-Stage Pipeline

Each of the 57 working papers in scope moves through the [[concepts/four-stage-pipeline]]:

| Stage | Output | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1. How-To Document | Formatted `.docx` (V5 template) | Field-by-field completion guide with ISA-aligned "Key Questions" table, cross-referenced against the [[entities/probe-audit-manual]]. Produced using the standardised [[concepts/ooxml-document-structure]]. |
| 2. Visual Script | Script document | Narrated presenter script with production cues and section transitions for screen recording. |
| 3. Visual Recording | Raw video | Screen-based walkthrough in CaseWare, recorded from the visual script. |
| 4. Professional Video | Polished video | Final edited training video for distribution on Success Community and YouTube. |

## IAASB Standards Coverage

The wiki includes 50 standards pages ingested from the IAASB 2025 Handbook (Volumes 1-5):

- **28 ISAs** (ISA 200 through ISA 810) — the core audit standards
- **2 ISQMs** (ISQM 1, ISQM 2) — firm-level quality management
- **ISA for LCE** — standalone standard for less complex entities
- **ISSA 5000** — sustainability assurance (effective Dec 2026)
- **2 ISREs** (2400, 2410) — review engagements
- **5 ISAEs** (3000, 3400, 3402, 3410, 3420) — assurance engagements
- **2 ISRSs** (4400, 4410) — related services

Each standard page includes objectives, key requirements, definitions, and mappings to specific Probe working papers. See [[concepts/isa-standards]] for the full ISA-to-working-paper map.

### Most-Referenced ISAs Across Working Papers

1. **[[standards/isa-315]]** — Identifying and Assessing Risks (11 working papers)
2. **[[standards/isa-240]]** — Fraud Responsibilities (10 working papers)
3. **[[standards/isa-330]]** — Auditor's Responses to Assessed Risks (6 working papers)
4. **[[standards/isa-220]]** — Quality Management for an Audit (6 working papers)
5. **[[standards/isa-320]]** — Materiality (5 working papers)

ISA 315 and ISA 240 together touch nearly every planning and risk assessment working paper, reflecting how pervasive risk identification and fraud awareness are throughout the audit process.
