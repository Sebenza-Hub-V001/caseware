# Success Community

The publishing platform where finalised How-To documents and professional videos are uploaded and approved for distribution. It serves as the primary delivery channel for completed training materials.

## Role in the Project

- **How-To documents** are uploaded after passing review (Stage 1 of the [[concepts/four-stage-pipeline]])
- **Professional videos** are uploaded after final editing (Stage 4)
- Content requires approval on the platform before it is considered fully published
- Videos are also uploaded to YouTube in parallel

## Publication Workflow

1. Document/video passes internal review
2. Uploaded to Success Community
3. Approved on Success Community
4. (Videos only) Also uploaded to YouTube
