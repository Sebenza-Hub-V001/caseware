# Probe Audit Premium+ Methodology

The Probe Audit Premium+ Methodology is the audit methodology implemented within [[entities/caseware-working-papers]]. It provides a structured approach to conducting audits in compliance with [[concepts/isa-standards]].

## Role in the Project

- Defines the working paper templates that form the basis of all How-To documents.
- The [[entities/probe-audit-manual]] provides the detailed methodology guidance used during the V2 enhancement stage.
- Each working paper maps to specific ISA requirements through this methodology.
