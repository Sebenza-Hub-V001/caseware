# CaseWare Probe How-To Document Project — Overview

> Source: User-provided project briefing, 2026-04-07

## What It Is

You're building a comprehensive library of **How-To training guides** for the **Probe Audit Premium+ Methodology** used within **CaseWare Working Papers**. These are professional reference documents that walk audit clerks (like yourself) through each working paper step by step — explaining what the document is for, how to complete it, and what to look out for.

## The Three-Stage Pipeline

Each working paper goes through a structured production pipeline:

**Stage 1 — How-To Document (V1)**
The base document. Created from the original CaseView working paper PDF. It covers the purpose of the working paper, field-by-field completion guidance, navigation tips, and practical notes. These are produced as professionally formatted `.docx` files using a specific OOXML structure you've standardised (Montserrat headings, custom colour palette, numbered sections, branded header/footer).

**Stage 2 — Enhanced How-To Document (V2)**
The V1 gets enhanced with a **"Key Questions — Additional Guidance"** table. This is built by cross-referencing the working paper against the relevant chapter of the **Probe Audit Manual**. The table identifies the 3–5 most critical questions in the paper and adds three columns: the question text, why it matters (linked to ISA standards), and what to document. This turns a basic how-to into something with real audit intelligence.

**Stage 3 — Video Script**
The V2 document is then converted into a **narrated presenter script** for recording screen-based walkthrough videos of the working paper in CaseWare. These scripts include production cues, section transitions, and a Video Details metadata table — ready for you to sit down and record.

## Standards & Methodology Alignment

The documents are aligned to **International Standards on Auditing (ISA)** as implemented through the Probe Audit Premium+ Methodology. Each working paper maps to specific ISA requirements (e.g., ISA 450 for evaluation of misstatements, ISA 315 for risk assessment). The V2 enhancement stage is where this alignment becomes explicit.

## Working Papers Covered

You've been working through these systematically by their CaseWare reference numbers (e.g., 02.40 Evaluation of Misstatements, 12.50, etc.). Each has a corresponding CaseView PDF that serves as the source input.
