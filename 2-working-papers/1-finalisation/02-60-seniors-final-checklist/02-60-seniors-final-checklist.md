# Senior's Final Checklist

## Purpose

The Senior's Final Checklist (02.60) is a comprehensive 52-question checklist completed by the senior (audit supervisor) to ensure that all audit procedures have been properly completed, documented, and concluded before the engagement is passed up for manager and partner review. It covers the full scope of the audit from contracts and compliance through to documentation quality and file assembly.

## Key Fields & Sections

### Considerations (Yes/No, Performed by, Ref)

**Engagement-level procedures (1-16):**
1. Major contracts and agreements reviewed
2. Compliance with laws and regulations considered
3. Internal control deficiencies appropriately considered
4. Audit programs reviewed for completeness and documentation
5. Opening balances verified and accounting policies consistently applied
6. Overall presentation of financial statements evaluated per IFRS for SMEs
7. Financial statements disclose all material facts
8. Legal representations obtained if applicable
9. Two-way communication with directors adequate
10. Reportable matters reported to management/directors (including fraud)
11. Sufficient evidence of discussions with directors
12. Management representations obtained covering:
    - (a) Responsibility for financial statement preparation
    - (b) All relevant information and access provided
    - (c) Related parties and transactions disclosed
    - (d) Representations required by other ISAs
    - (e) Any additional representations deemed necessary
13. Date of written representation appropriate
14. Representation in proper written form
15. Concerns about reliability of representations addressed
16. Action taken if management did not provide representations

**Misstatements and adjustments (17-18):**
17. Materiality of uncorrected differences considered (individually and in aggregate), including size/nature and prior period effects
18. Reasons for adjustments considered for potential fraud indicators

**Subsequent events and going concern (19-21):**
19. Specific procedures to identify significant events between balance sheet date and report date
20. Accounting treatment of post-balance-sheet events correct
21. Going concern concept properly assessed

**Final analytical review (22):**
22. Final analytical review performed and results consistent with understanding of business

**Documentation quality (23-29, 39-42):**
23. Inconsistencies in management/director enquiry responses investigated
24. Changes in assurance level dealt with per ISA 210
25. Audit documentation sufficient for an experienced auditor to understand: identifying characteristics, nature/timing/extent of procedures, who performed/reviewed work
26. Details of significant discussions with management/directors documented
27. Resolution of significant inconsistencies documented
28. All material transactions and balances audited
29. Conclusions documented and related to assertions tested

**Fraud and non-compliance (30-36):**
30. Documents inspected appeared genuine
31. Able to conclude whether financial statements materially misstated due to fraud
32. Suspected fraud communicated to directors/management
33. Understanding of all instances of non-compliance obtained
34. Non-compliance assessed for management response appropriateness
35. Suspected non-compliance communicated to directors/management
36. Implications of non-compliance evaluated

**Final assessments (37-38):**
37. Risk assessment at assertion level still appropriate
38. Materiality assessment still appropriate

**File quality and completion (39-52):**
39. Working papers sufficient for understanding by an unconnected auditor
40. Working papers properly indexed and cross-referenced
41. Proper lead schedules agreeing to trial balance and financial statements
42. All outstanding items cleared
43. Regulatory reporting completed (reportable irregularities, money laundering under FICA)
44. Professional judgment and scepticism exercised throughout
45. Inconsistent information properly addressed
46. Engagement team understood and complied with all relevant ISAs
47. Sufficient appropriate audit evidence to reduce audit risk to acceptably low level
48. No new procedures or conclusions after date of auditor's report
49. No changes to audit documentation (other than administrative) after report date
50. Audit documentation prepared on a timely basis; file assembly and lockdown per SoQM
51. Board minutes inspected for financial statement approval (Companies Act s30(3)(c))
52. Financial statements inspected for authorised director signature

## Completion Guidance

- This is a 3-page, 52-question checklist -- every question must be addressed (Yes/No/N/A)
- The Performed by and Ref columns must be populated for each item
- This checklist should be completed before the Manager's Final Checklist (02.70)
- Questions 47-52 are particularly critical as they deal with post-report-date documentation discipline
- The checklist serves as a quality control checkpoint before the file progresses to manager review

## How-To Document Summary

The How-To V5 document for 02.60 Senior's Final Checklist explains the functionality and features of this CaseView checklist document within Probe Audit Premium+. It describes the 52 pre-populated considerations that the senior member of the engagement team must confirm before finalising the engagement.

### Key Topics Addressed

- **Document placement** -- located in the Finalisation folder in CaseWare document manager
- **Considerations** -- 52 pre-populated questions covering all aspects of the audit: contracts and agreements, compliance with laws and regulations, internal control deficiencies, audit programs, opening balances, financial statement presentation, representations, misstatements, subsequent events, going concern, documentation quality, fraud, and file assembly
- **Input columns** -- Yes/No (all should be answered Yes before finalisation), Performed by (name/initials and date), and Ref (cross-reference to supporting working papers)
- **First-level quality assurance** -- serves as evidence that the senior has reviewed all critical aspects of the engagement including ISA compliance, documentation adequacy, communications with management and those charged with governance, and audit opinion appropriateness
- **Document outcomes** -- all 52 considerations must be addressed; where answered No, the matter must be resolved before file assembly and lockdown
- **Group audits** -- checklist applies in the context of the group audit, including component auditor involvement and group audit documentation adequacy
