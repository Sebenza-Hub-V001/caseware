# Minutes

## Purpose

The Minutes working paper (01.60) provides template minutes for two key corporate governance meetings: the Board of Directors meeting (for approval of the annual financial statements) and the Annual General Meeting (AGM). These minutes document the formal corporate decisions required by the Companies Act before the financial statements can be signed and the auditor's report issued.

## Key Fields & Sections

### Minutes of the Meeting of the Director (Board Meeting)

1. **Present** -- Names and signatures of directors present
2. **Annual Financial Statements** -- Resolution approving the annual financial statements for the financial year (as to contents and form)
3. **Report of the Director** -- Resolution approving the form and content of the director's report
4. **Signatories** -- Resolution authorising specific person(s) to sign the financial statements and director's report on behalf of the director
5. **Date of Annual General Meeting** -- Resolution setting the date of the forthcoming AGM
- Chairperson sign-off (Name, Signature, Date)

### Minutes of the Annual General Meeting (AGM)

6. **Present** -- Names and signatures of shareholders (up to 4 shareholder slots)
7. **Waiver of Notice** -- Note that waiver signed by all persons entitled to vote; meeting declared duly constituted
8. **Minutes of Previous AGM** -- Confirmation that previous minutes were duly signed
9. **Annual Financial Statements** -- Presentation of financial statements including Director's report and audit committee report; auditors' report taken as read; unanimous adoption
10. **Directorate** -- Record of directors retaining office (slots for names)
11. **Dividends** -- Resolution on dividend recommendation (standard: no dividends declared)
12. **Remuneration of the Director** -- Confirmation of director remuneration as shown in financial statements
13. **Appointment of Auditor** -- Recording of secretarial/accountancy duties performed; shareholder agreement to auditor appointment; deemed reappointment until next AGM
14. **Appointment of the Audit Committee** -- Directors appointed to audit committee; confirmation of compliance with Companies Act s94(5) and Regulation 42
15. **General Authority to Allot or Issue Shares** -- General authority given to director for unissued shares (valid until next AGM)
- Chairperson sign-off (Name, Signature, Date)

## ISA References

- **Companies Act 71 of 2008, s30(3)(c)** -- Board approval and authorisation of financial statements
- **Companies Act 71 of 2008, s94(5)** -- Audit committee requirements
- **Companies Regulations, 2011, Regulation 42** -- Audit committee composition
- **ISA 560** -- Subsequent Events (date of approval of financial statements is relevant)
- **ISA 700.48** -- Report date (no earlier than date of sufficient appropriate evidence, including director approval)

## Completion Guidance

- These are template minutes that must be customised for the specific entity
- The board meeting minutes must be completed before the auditor can date the auditor's report (per ISA 700.48)
- All name fields, dates, and shareholder details must be populated
- The auditor inspects these minutes as part of the Senior's Final Checklist (02.60, questions 51-52) to confirm board approval
- The AGM minutes may be completed after the audit report date, but the board meeting minutes must precede it
- This is a 3-page document (board meeting on page 1, AGM on pages 2-3)
