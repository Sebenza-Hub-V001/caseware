# Engagement Team Declaration

## Purpose

The Engagement Team Declaration (02.05) is a single-page document where each member of the engagement team formally confirms their compliance with ethical requirements, independence, and confidentiality obligations for the audit engagement. It serves as documented evidence that all team members have met their individual ethical responsibilities.

## Key Fields & Sections

### Firm Personnel Declaration
Each team member confirms the following four statements:
1. Compliance with relevant ethical requirements applicable to the engagement and the firm's related SoQM policies and procedures
2. No breaches of relevant ethical requirements were identified on this engagement
3. Remained independent of the client throughout the engagement
4. Will continue to keep information obtained during audit procedures confidential

### Sign-Off Table
For each team member:
- Name
- Role
- Comment (for any notes or qualifications)
- Signature
- Date

## ISA References

- **ISA 220** -- Quality management for an audit of financial statements (ethical requirements and independence)
- **IESBA Code of Ethics** -- International Code of Ethics for Professional Accountants (independence and confidentiality)

## Completion Guidance

- Every member of the engagement team must sign this declaration
- Should be completed at or near the end of the engagement (finalisation phase)
- If any team member identifies a breach of ethical requirements, this must be noted in the Comment column and escalated appropriately
- The declaration covers the entire engagement period, not just the finalisation phase
- This is a one-page document -- keep it concise and ensure all rows are completed

## How-To Document Summary

The How-To V5 document for 02.05 Engagement Team Declaration explains the functionality and features of this CaseView declaration document within Probe Audit Premium+. It describes how each engagement team member formally confirms compliance with ethical requirements, independence, and confidentiality obligations.

### Key Topics Addressed

- **Document placement** -- located in the Finalisation folder in CaseWare document manager
- **Declaration statements** -- four pre-populated statements addressing: (1) compliance with relevant ethical requirements and the firm's SoQM policies and procedures, (2) no breaches of relevant ethical requirements identified, (3) independence from the client, (4) confidentiality of information obtained during the engagement
- **Sign-off fields** -- Name, Role, Comment, Signature, and Date for each team member
- **Document outcomes** -- all engagement team members must complete the declaration; additional rows may be inserted to accommodate the full team
- **Group audits** -- declaration applies to all members of the group engagement team when engagement type is Group Audit Single or Group Audit Multiple

### ISA References in How-To

- ISA 220 (Quality Management for an Audit of Financial Statements)
- ISQM 1 (Quality Management for Firms)

### Document Features

- Insert or delete a comment, insert or delete a row at the end, guidance pop-ups, freeze functionality
