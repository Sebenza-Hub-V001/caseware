# Overall Analytical Review

## Purpose

The Overall Analytical Review (02.30) is a finalisation-phase analytical procedure performed at or near the end of the audit. It presents key financial metrics for the current and prior year with percentage changes, enabling the engagement team to form an overall conclusion about whether the financial statements are consistent with the auditor's understanding of the entity and to identify any previously unrecognised risks of material misstatement.

## Key Fields & Sections

### Income Statement Metrics
- **Revenue** -- Current year vs prior year with % change
- **Cost of sales** -- Current year vs prior year with % change
- **Gross profit** -- Amount and % change
- **Gross profit percentage** -- Current year vs prior year
- **Other income** -- Amount and % change
- **Overheads** -- Amount and % change
- **Overheads and tax** -- Combined amount with % change
- **Tax** -- Amount and % change
- **Net profit before tax** -- Amount and % change
- **Net profit after tax** -- Amount and % change
- **Net profit margin** -- Current year vs prior year with % change

### Balance Sheet Metrics
- **Current assets** -- Amount and % change
- **Non-current assets** -- Amount and % change
- **Total assets** -- Amount and % change
- **Current liabilities** -- Amount and % change
- **Non-current liabilities** -- Amount and % change
- **Total liabilities** -- Amount and % change
- **Equity** -- Amount and % change

### Financial Ratios
- **Current ratio** -- Current year vs prior year with % change
- **Asset turnover** -- Current year vs prior year
- **Return on assets** -- Current year vs prior year with % change
- **Return on equity** -- Current year vs prior year with % change
- **Debt / equity ratio** -- Current year vs prior year with % change

## ISA References

- **ISA 520** -- Analytical Procedures (specifically ISA 520.6 -- analytical procedures near the end of the audit)
- **ISA 315** -- Identifying and Assessing the Risks of Material Misstatement (understanding the entity)

## Completion Guidance

- This is an auto-populated document -- CaseWare pulls the financial data from the trial balance / financial statements
- The auditor's role is to review the ratios and percentage changes and investigate any unexpected fluctuations or anomalies
- Commentary should be added to explain significant changes (the document provides the data; the auditor provides the interpretation)
- Results feed directly into the Going Concern Conclusion (02.20) -- particularly cash flow and liquidity indicators
- Any unexpected findings may require additional audit procedures or reconsideration of risk assessments
- This is a 2-page document with a structured tabular layout

## How-To Document Summary

The How-To V5 document for 02.30 Overall Analytical Review explains the functionality and features of this CaseView summary document within Probe Audit Premium+. It describes how the document presents pre-populated financial metrics and ratios for the engagement team to perform an overall analytical review at the conclusion of the audit.

### Key Topics Addressed

- **Document placement** -- located in the Finalisation folder in CaseWare document manager
- **Sign-off fields** -- Planning by, Reviewed by, Performed by, and Final review fields for recording team members responsible for the analytical review
- **Financial metrics** -- all auto-populated from the engagement file data, including:
  - Income statement items: Revenue, Gross profit, Cost of sales, Other income, Overheads, Net profit before and after tax, Net profit margin
  - Balance sheet items: Current assets, Non-current assets, Total assets, Current liabilities, Non-current liabilities, Total liabilities, Equity
  - Financial ratios: Return on assets, Return on equity, Asset turnover, Current ratio, Debt/equity ratio
- **Current year, Prior year, and % Change columns** -- all auto-calculated; no input required
- **Document outcomes** -- engagement team reviews percentage changes for consistency with understanding of the entity and identifies significant or unusual fluctuations requiring further investigation
- **Group audits** -- analytical review applies at both group and component level when engagement type is Group Audit Single or Group Audit Multiple
