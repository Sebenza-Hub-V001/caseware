# IAASB 2025 Handbook

## Summary

The IAASB 2025 Handbook is the official compilation of international standards on quality management, auditing, review, other assurance, and related services, published by the International Auditing and Assurance Standards Board (IAASB) in September 2025. It serves as the authoritative reference for all pronouncements in effect or approved as of that date, spanning five volumes that cover the full scope of IAASB standards from quality management through to sustainability assurance.

## Volumes

## Key Claims

- This is the 2025 edition, published September 2025
- Contains newly effective standards: ISA for LCE (effective for audits of financial statements for periods beginning on or after 15 December 2025) and ISSA 5000 (effective for sustainability assurance engagements for periods beginning on or after 15 December 2026)
- ISAE 3410 (Assurance Engagements on Greenhouse Gas Statements) is marked as superseded/withdrawn, effective 15 December 2026, when ISSA 5000 takes its place
- Volume 1 contains the bulk of the content (1,046 of ~1,725 total pages), reflecting the primacy of audit standards in practice
