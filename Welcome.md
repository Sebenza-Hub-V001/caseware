# Welcome to Sebenza Hub

A personal knowledge base for the **CaseWare Probe How-To Document Project** — building a complete library of professional training guides for the Probe audit methodology.

---

## The Audit Journey

This vault is organised to follow the audit engagement lifecycle:

### 1-standards/ — Know the Rules
The regulatory foundation. 50 IAASB standards (ISAs, ISQMs, ISREs, ISAEs, ISRSs) with requirements, definitions, and mappings to working papers.

### 2-working-papers/ — Do the Work
The core audit work. 39 working paper pages organised by engagement phase:
- **Pre-Engagement Planning** (10.xx) — Engagement evaluation through materiality
- **Planning & Risk Assessment** (11.xx) — Risk analysis, fraud, internal controls
- **Audit Planning** (12.xx) — Strategy, memorandum, quality review
- **Execution** (16-18.xx) — Accounting records, opening balances, financial statements
- **Finalisation** (01-02.xx) — Sign-off, subsequent events, going concern, reporting

### 3-pipeline/ — Produce the Content
The four-stage production pipeline tracking How-To documents through to professional videos. See [[3-pipeline/index]] for current progress.
