# ISA 402 — Audit Considerations Relating to an Entity Using a Service Organization

## Objective

The objectives of the user auditor, when the user entity uses the services of a service organization, are: (Para. 7)
- To obtain an understanding of the nature and significance of the services provided by the service organization and their effect on the user entity's system of internal control sufficient to provide an appropriate basis for the identification and assessment of the risks of material misstatement; and
- To design and perform audit procedures responsive to those risks.

## Scope

ISA 402 deals with the user auditor's responsibility to obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence when a user entity uses the services of one or more service organizations. It expands on how the user auditor applies [[1-standards/2-isa/isa-315]] and [[1-standards/2-isa/isa-330]] in obtaining an understanding of the user entity (including internal control) sufficient to identify and assess risks, and in designing further audit procedures responsive to those risks. The standard does not apply to services by financial institutions limited to processing specifically authorized transactions (e.g. checking account or securities transactions), nor to audits of transactions arising from proprietary financial interests.

## Key Requirements

### Understanding Services Provided by a Service Organization (Para. 9-14)
- The user auditor shall obtain an understanding of how the user entity uses the service organization, including:
  - Nature and significance of services and their effect on internal control
  - Nature and materiality of transactions processed or accounts affected
  - Degree of interaction between service organization and user entity activities
  - Nature of the relationship, including relevant contractual terms
- The user auditor shall identify controls at the user entity relating to services provided by the service organization and evaluate their design and implementation.
- The user auditor shall determine whether a sufficient understanding has been obtained to provide an appropriate basis for risk identification and assessment.
- If insufficient understanding is obtained from the user entity, the user auditor shall obtain it through: (a) a type 1 or type 2 report; (b) contacting the service organization through the user entity; (c) visiting the service organization; or (d) using another auditor.

### Using Type 1 or Type 2 Reports (Para. 13-14)
- The user auditor shall be satisfied as to the service auditor's professional competence and independence, and the adequacy of the standards under which the report was issued.
- The user auditor shall evaluate whether the description and design of controls is at an appropriate date/period, evaluate the sufficiency of evidence provided, and determine whether complementary user entity controls are relevant.

### Responding to Assessed Risks (Para. 15-17)
- The user auditor shall determine whether sufficient appropriate audit evidence is available from records at the user entity; if not, perform further procedures or use another auditor at the service organization.
- **Tests of controls** (Para. 16): When reliance on service organization controls is planned, evidence of operating effectiveness shall be obtained through: (a) a type 2 report; (b) performing tests of controls at the service organization; or (c) using another auditor.
- **Using a type 2 report** (Para. 17): The user auditor shall evaluate the description, design and operating effectiveness of controls for appropriate date/period, determine relevance of complementary user entity controls, evaluate the adequacy of the time period covered, and evaluate the relevance of tests and results to the user entity's assertions.

### Subservice Organizations (Para. 18)
- If a type 1 or type 2 report excludes services of a subservice organization relevant to the audit, the user auditor shall apply the requirements of ISA 402 with respect to those services.

### Fraud, Non-Compliance and Uncorrected Misstatements (Para. 19)
- The user auditor shall inquire of management whether the service organization has reported any fraud, non-compliance with laws and regulations, or uncorrected misstatements affecting the user entity's financial statements, and evaluate how such matters affect further audit procedures.

### Reporting (Para. 20-22)
- The user auditor shall modify the opinion per ISA 705 (Revised) if unable to obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence regarding services relevant to the audit.
- The user auditor shall not refer to the work of a service auditor in an unmodified opinion unless required by law or regulation.
- If reference to the service auditor's work is relevant to understanding a modification, the report shall indicate this does not diminish the user auditor's responsibility.

## Key Definitions

- **Complementary user entity controls** — Controls that the service organization assumes will be implemented by user entities, identified in the description of its system
- **Type 1 report** — Report on the description and design of controls at a service organization at a specified date, including the service auditor's opinion on the description and suitability of design
- **Type 2 report** — Report on the description, design, and operating effectiveness of controls at a service organization for a specified period, including the service auditor's opinion and description of tests and results
- **Service auditor** — An auditor who provides an assurance report on the controls of a service organization
- **Service organization** — A third-party organization that provides services to user entities that are part of those entities' information systems relevant to financial reporting
- **Service organization's system** — Policies and procedures designed and maintained by the service organization to provide services covered by the service auditor's report
- **Subservice organization** — A service organization used by another service organization to perform some services provided to user entities
- **User auditor** — An auditor who audits and reports on the financial statements of a user entity
- **User entity** — An entity that uses a service organization and whose financial statements are being audited
