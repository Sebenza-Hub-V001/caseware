# ISAE 3410 — Assurance Engagements on Greenhouse Gas Statements (Withdrawn)

> **CAUTION:** ISAE 3410 has been withdrawn with effect from **December 15, 2026**, the effective date of [[1-standards/issa-5000]]. It is superseded by ISSA 5000 for sustainability assurance engagements.

## Objective

The objectives of the practitioner are:

1. To obtain either reasonable assurance or limited assurance, as appropriate, about whether the GHG statement is free from material misstatement, whether due to fraud or error, thereby enabling the practitioner to express a conclusion;
2. To report, in accordance with the practitioner's findings, about whether:
   - (Reasonable assurance) The GHG statement is prepared, in all material respects, in accordance with the applicable criteria; or
   - (Limited assurance) Anything has come to the practitioner's attention that causes the practitioner to believe the GHG statement is not prepared, in all material respects, in accordance with the applicable criteria; and
3. To communicate as otherwise required by this ISAE, in accordance with the practitioner's findings.

## Scope

This ISAE deals with assurance engagements to report on an entity's **greenhouse gas (GHG) statement**. It covers both reasonable and limited assurance engagements.

Key scope points:
- Applies to GHG statements prepared as part of a regulatory disclosure regime, emissions trading scheme, or voluntary disclosures
- When the practitioner's conclusion covers information beyond a GHG statement (e.g., a broader sustainability report), this ISAE applies to the GHG statement portion unless it is a relatively minor part
- Where local laws or emissions trading scheme requirements differ, compliance with those does not automatically mean compliance with this ISAE
- The practitioner must comply with [[1-standards/isae-3000]] in addition to this ISAE

Effective for assurance reports covering periods ending on or after **September 30, 2013**. Withdrawn with effect from December 15, 2026.

## Key Requirements

1. **ISAE 3000 (Revised) compliance** — Apply alongside this ISAE
2. **Acceptance and continuance** — Evaluate competence, criteria suitability, entity cooperation
3. **Planning** — Develop overall strategy and engagement plan
4. **Materiality** — Determine materiality for planning and performing the engagement, including performance materiality
5. **Understanding the entity** — Understand the entity's environment, internal controls, emissions sources, quantification methods, data flows, organizational and operational boundaries
6. **Risk assessment** — Identify and assess risks of material misstatement in the GHG statement
7. **Responding to risks** — Design and perform procedures responsive to assessed risks, including:
   - Tests of controls (reasonable assurance) or inquiry/analytical procedures (limited assurance)
   - Substantive procedures on emissions data, emission factors, calculations
   - Evaluating estimates and assumptions
8. **Using work of other practitioners** — Consider when other practitioners' work is used
9. **Written representations** — Obtain from management
10. **Subsequent events and comparative information** — Address appropriately
11. **Documentation** — Document the engagement
12. **Forming conclusion** — Form and express assurance conclusion
13. **Reporting** — Prepare assurance report

## Key Definitions

- **GHG statement** — A statement that quantifies emissions, removals, and emissions deductions for a specified period
- **Emissions** — GHG emitted into the atmosphere (Scope 1 direct, Scope 2 energy indirect, Scope 3 other indirect)
- **Removals** — GHG removed from the atmosphere
- **Emissions deductions** — Reductions in emissions (e.g., through offsets or renewable energy certificates)
- **Emission factor** — A factor that converts activity data into GHG emissions data

## Application to Probe Working Papers

This standard is not currently referenced by any Probe working papers. Given its withdrawal effective December 2026, any new development for GHG/sustainability assurance should reference [[1-standards/issa-5000]] instead.
