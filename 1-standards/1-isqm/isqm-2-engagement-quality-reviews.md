# ISQM 2 — Engagement Quality Reviews

## Objective

The objective of the firm, through appointing an eligible engagement quality reviewer, is to perform an objective evaluation of the significant judgments made by the engagement team and the conclusions reached thereon.

## Scope

ISQM 2 deals with the appointment and eligibility of the engagement quality reviewer, and the engagement quality reviewer's responsibilities relating to the performance and documentation of an engagement quality review. It applies to all engagements for which an engagement quality review is required under [[1-standards/1-isqm/isqm-1-quality-management-firms]]. The standard is premised on the firm being subject to ISQM 1.

## Key Requirements

- **Appointment responsibility:** The firm shall assign responsibility for appointing engagement quality reviewers to an individual(s) with the competence, capabilities and appropriate authority.
- **Eligibility criteria:** The engagement quality reviewer shall not be a member of the engagement team and must have competence, capabilities, sufficient time, and appropriate authority; comply with relevant ethical requirements including independence; and comply with applicable law and regulation.
- **Cooling-off period:** A two-year cooling-off period (or longer if required by ethical requirements) before an engagement partner can become the engagement quality reviewer for the same engagement.
- **Performance of the review:** The engagement quality reviewer shall perform an objective evaluation of the significant judgments made by the engagement team and the conclusions reached thereon.
- **Documentation:** The engagement quality review shall be documented, including the basis for the reviewer's conclusions.
- **Independence:** The review is not intended to be an evaluation of whether the entire engagement complies with all professional standards — it focuses on significant judgments and conclusions.

## Key Definitions

- **Engagement quality review** — An objective evaluation of the significant judgments made by the engagement team and the conclusions reached thereon, performed by the engagement quality reviewer and completed on or before the date of the engagement report.
- **Engagement quality reviewer** — A partner, other individual in the firm, or an external individual, appointed by the firm to perform the engagement quality review.
- **Relevant ethical requirements** — Principles of professional ethics and ethical requirements applicable to a professional accountant when undertaking the engagement quality review.

## Application to Probe Working Papers

ISQM 2 is a firm-level standard governing the quality review process. In the Probe methodology, engagement quality reviews are facilitated through the completion and sign-off procedures in the CaseWare file. Working papers such as [[2-working-papers/1-finalisation/02-80-eqr-execution-finalisation/02-80-eqr-execution-finalisation]] (referenced by [[1-standards/2-isa/isa-220-quality-management-audit]]) support the documentation of quality management including engagement quality reviews.
